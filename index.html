Track & Field Meet

18 & 19 January 2019

Te Anau

On 18 & 19 January 2019 Fiordland Athletics Club is hosting a track and field meet. This event is open to everyone and you do not need to be a member of a club to participate. Come along, give it a go and have a good time.
If you are over 16 you can enter any event. If you are under 16 you can participate in the long jump, 60m and shotput.
For those who are interested there will likely be a social bush run on Sunday 20 January.
Friday night will be a throws pentathlon, which will start at 6pm. The other events will take place on Saturday. To enter simply email your entry to the email address provided below.

Condition of Entry:
Everyone is welcome
